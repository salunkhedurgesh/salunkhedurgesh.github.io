Cuspidal Robots

1. What are cuspidal robots?


-> Cuspidal robots are those robots which can go from one inverse kinematic solution to another without crossing singularities in the joint space!

These robots have multiple inverse kinematic solutions in an aspect. An aspect is the largest singularity free region in the joint space of a serial robot.
